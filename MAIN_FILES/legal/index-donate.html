Our Details

The concept is driven by Donor Support. Organized groups of unemployed people scan community needs and develop relevant Community Solution Projects-CSPs that respond to the needs. The groups then seek donors to provide resources required to execute the CSPs. The resources may be anything (e.g money, transport, equipment, etc).
